Questions

What's included in the free tier?

Manual video and photo culling is completely free with no limits. You also get 3 auto-edit projects to try the full editorial experience.

Can I switch plans?

Yes. Upgrade or downgrade anytime through the Mac App Store. Changes take effect at your next billing cycle.

Is there a free trial?

Every paid plan includes a 7-day free trial. Cancel anytime during the trial and you won't be charged.

What happens to my projects if I downgrade?

All your existing projects and exports are yours to keep. You just won't be able to create new auto-edit projects beyond your new tier's limits.

Is this macOS only?

Yes. CineBuild is a native macOS application built with SwiftUI and optimized for Apple Silicon. There are no plans for other platforms.

Does the annual plan save money?

Yes — 33% off compared to monthly billing across all tiers.
